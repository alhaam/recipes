Ingredients:

 - 4 to 5 hot peppers
 - 1 or 2 carrots
 - 1 or 1/2 garlic clove
 - 1 cup water
 - Salt to taste
 - 1 cup vinegar

Directions: 

 1. Mix the water and vinegar in a container.
 2. Boil the hot peppers carrot and garlic in the vinegar for about 10-15 minutes. 
 3. Let the mix to cool down. 
 4. Blend the mix untill it becomes like sauce,  add salt as needed.
